Appetizers That Set the Tone

Shareable • Flavor-Forward

Appetizers are a major part of the Outback experience. Starters are built to prepare guests for the main course with rich flavors and satisfying textures. The iconic Blooming Onion is the signature pick many diners associate with the brand, while other fried and grilled options are perfect for groups who want to share.

  • Signature Starter Great when you want a bold first bite and something shareable for the table.
  • Group-Friendly Ordering Best strategy: order 1–2 appetizers per 3–4 people to sample multiple flavors.

Steak Selection and Cooking Style

Cooked-to-Order • Consistent Quality

Steaks are the heart of the Outback Steakhouse Menu US. High-quality cuts are seasoned with a proprietary spice blend and grilled to your preferred doneness—from rare to well-done. Favorites typically include sirloin, ribeye, filet mignon, and New York strip. Each steak is paired with satisfying sides that elevate the overall meal.

Popular Steak Choices

Sirloin • Ribeye • Filet Mignon • New York Strip — select based on tenderness vs. rich marbling.

How to Choose Doneness

If you like juicy + tender, go medium-rare to medium. Prefer firmer texture? Choose medium-well to well-done.

Chicken, Ribs, and Combination Meals

Variety • Great Value

Not everyone orders steak, and the menu supports that with flavorful grilled chicken and slow-cooked ribs. Ribs are typically coated in signature sauces that bring smoky richness. Combination meals are a favorite because they let you enjoy more than one protein on a single plate—ideal when you want variety without ordering two full entrees.

Seafood Options for Balanced Dining

Light • Satisfying

Seafood adds a balanced option for guests who want something lighter than steak but still full of flavor. Common picks include grilled salmon, shrimp, and lobster tail. These meals are usually seasoned simply to preserve natural taste and served with fresh sides.

Best for a lighter meal

Seafood + vegetables is a clean, satisfying combination for lunch or dinner.

Best for special occasions

Lobster tail-style upgrades can make your meal feel more premium without overcomplicating the order.

Side Dishes and Meal Customization

Customize • Big Portions

Side dishes play a major role in the overall Outback experience. Guests often choose from mashed potatoes, baked potatoes, seasoned rice, fresh vegetables, and other classic comfort sides. Portion sizes are generous, so sides feel like a real part of the meal—not an afterthought.

  • Classic Comfort Sides Mashed potatoes, baked potatoes, rice, and vegetables help you tailor the meal to your taste.
  • Smart Customization Pick your main first, then choose sides to match your mood: hearty, balanced, or lighter.
